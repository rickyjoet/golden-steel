CLASSES

Classes are held at the International District Chinatown Community Center in Seattle, Washington.
We teach a variety of classic kung-fu fighting techniques through the neoclassical style of Baat Ying Batt Faat (Eight Animals and Eight Methods).
By blending traditional kicking, striking, and grappling methods with modern fighting theories, our Si-Jo (founder) Allen Chinn
has created a system of Chinese Martial arts that respects tradition while staying relevant for today's practitioner.
