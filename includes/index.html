Welcome to the Golden Steel Athletic Association website.
We teach a variety of kung-fu fighting techniques through the neoclassical
style of Baat Ying Batt Faat (Eight Animals and Eight Methods). By blending
traditional kicking, striking, and grappling with modern fighting theories, our
style respects traditional Chinese Martial Arts while
staying relevant for today's practitioner.
